WHAT WE DO

SurveillanceMaster play a crucial role in monitoring and managing surveillance systems to ensure the safety and security of an area or facility

Monitoring

We continuously observe live surveillance camera feeds to detect and assess any suspicious activities, security breaches, or safety hazards. They remain vigilant and attentive to identify potential threats or risks in real-time.

Operating Surveillance Systems

We are responsible for operating and controlling the surveillance equipment, including pan-tilt-zoom (PTZ) cameras, digital video recorders (DVRs), monitors, and other specialized surveillance technologies.

Recording and Archiving

We manage the recording and archiving of video footage. They ensure that recording systems are functioning correctly, monitor storage capacities, and initiate appropriate measures to store and retrieve video data for investigative or evidentiary purposes.

Technical Troubleshooting

In case of equipment malfunctions or technical issues, we perform initial troubleshooting and attempt to resolve minor problems. We may coordinate with technical support or maintenance personnel for more complex repairs or system upgrades.

Access Control System

We Implement advanced access control solutions to enhance security and restrict unauthorized access.Utilizing technologies such as key cards, biometric authentication, and centralized control systems.

Communication and Collaboration

We provide timely information, communicate incidents or observations, and collaborate effectively to address security concerns or emergency situations.

Fire Alarm

We Offer monitoring and responding to fire alarm systems. They receive and assess alarm signals, coordinate emergency responses, and communicate with relevant authorities. Their role is crucial in ensuring prompt and effective actions during fire emergencies..

Solar Panel

We Offer monitoring and maintaining solar power systems. They perform regular inspections, troubleshoot issues, and ensure optimal system performance. Their tasks include cleaning panels, analyzing performance data, and implementing necessary maintenance and repairs.

PABX (Private Branch Exchange)

We handle incoming and outgoing calls, route calls to the appropriate extensions or departments, and provide assistance to callers. They are responsible for maintaining effective communication within the organization using the PABX system.

UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply)

We monitor the UPS system, perform regular maintenance checks, and troubleshoot any issues that may arise. Their role is crucial in ensuring continuous power availability and protecting sensitive equipment from power disruptions.
